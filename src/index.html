What is LuckyReflections

Lucky Reflections is a fair launch ERC-20 token with reflection tokenomics Simply hold LR and receive more rewards based on the amount of LR you hold and the trading volume of that particular day.

LuckyReflections Great Features:

6% Transaction
Fee

4% of every transaction will get evenly distributed amongst all LR token holders (Uniswap contract will not receive reflection rewards), 1% will burned forever reducing the circulating supply, and 1% will go to the developers which can be used for marketing as well as further development of the project.

Rewards Paid
Automatically

The reflection rewards will automatically go to your wallet, there is no need to claim and waste gas on transactions. The amount of reflections you receive is based upon the daily volume for that day

Provide Liquidity
On Uniswap

Provide liquidity to the Lucky Reflections token and receive the fees Uniswap generates. As a fair launched token liquidity providers have an opportunity to capitalize on the fees.

Secured By
Design

All LuckyReflection token smart contracts are audited by a third party company. You can check the audit report by clicking on the “audit” tab above.

What Makes Us
Different

LuckyReflection is not only a fair launch token, but it is also a fairly distributed reflection token. The Uniswap contract that will initially hold 90% of the tokens will be excluded from receiving reflection rewards. The first developer wallet will only have 10% and will also be excluded from receiving reflection rewards. This means the community will receive 100% even distribution of reflections based upon their holding of LR tokens.
